Frequently Asked Questions

    1. What is included in the tour package?
    Our tour packages typically include accommodation, meals, guided activities, and transportation within the destination. Specific inclusions are detailed for each tour on our website.

    2. Do I need prior hiking experience?
    Not at all! We offer tours suitable for all levels, from beginners to experienced hikers. Our guides will ensure you feel comfortable and prepared.

    3. What should I pack for the trip?
    We recommend warm layers, sturdy hiking boots, a waterproof jacket, and any personal essentials. A detailed packing list will be provided upon booking.

    4. Can I see the Northern Lights on every tour?
    While we can’t guarantee sightings, our winter tours are planned during the best seasons and in optimal locations for seeing the auroras.

    5. What is your cancellation policy?
    We offer a flexible cancellation policy. Please refer to the terms and conditions provided at the time of booking for full details.

    6. How do I book a tour?
    You can book directly on our website or contact our customer service team for assistance.
