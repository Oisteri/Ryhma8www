Discover The Magic Of Activities

Activities We Offer

    1. Cross-Country Skiing
    Explore scenic snowy trails through enchanting forests and pristine landscapes. Perfect for beginners and seasoned skiers alike, our ski routes offer breathtaking views and peaceful serenity.

    2. Downhill Skiing
    For adrenaline lovers, our nearby slopes provide the ultimate skiing experience. With well-maintained runs for all skill levels, you can enjoy the rush of speeding down snowy hills surrounded by stunning mountain vistas.

    3. SPA Experience
    Unwind and rejuvenate in our luxurious SPA facilities. From warm saunas to refreshing pools, our SPA provides the perfect retreat after a day of winter activities.

    4. Massage & Wellness Treatments
    Indulge in our range of professional massages and wellness treatments. Relax your body, mind, and soul with therapies designed to soothe and revitalize.

    5. Ice Swimming
    Embrace the unique Nordic tradition of ice swimming! Take a dip in our icy lake and feel the invigorating sensation followed by a warm-up session in our cozy sauna.

    6. BBQ and Campfire at the Laavu
    Gather with friends and family around our laavu (traditional Finnish shelter) for a memorable BBQ and campfire experience. Grill your favorite treats and enjoy the warmth of the fire under the starry sky.

    7. Hotel Amenities & Gift Shop
    Our hotel offers a wide range of services to make your stay comfortable, including a delicious restaurant, cozy lounge areas, and a gift shop filled with local crafts, souvenirs, and unique finds.
